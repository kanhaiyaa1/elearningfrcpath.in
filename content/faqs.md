## Frequently Asked Questions

### What exams do your courses cover?
We cover **FRCPath Part 1 and Part 2** (Royal College of Pathologists, UK) and **NEET SS Pathology** (India's super-specialty entrance exam for Pathology).

### Who are the courses suitable for?
Our courses are designed for postgraduate pathology trainees, residents, and doctors preparing for specialist fellowship or super-specialty examinations.

### Are the lectures available on-demand?
Yes. All recorded video lectures are available on-demand so you can study at your own pace.

### How do I enroll?
Contact us at [info@elearningfrcpath.in](mailto:info@elearningfrcpath.in) to get enrollment details and course access.

### Is there support for doubt-clearing?
Yes, we offer doubt-clearing sessions as part of the course package.

### How often is the content updated?
Our content is regularly reviewed and updated to reflect the latest examination patterns and pathology guidelines, including WHO classification updates.

### Can I access the material on mobile?
Yes, the platform is accessible on all devices including smartphones and tablets.
