## Our Faculty

Our courses are designed and delivered by experienced pathologists with a strong background in both clinical practice and medical education.

### Expert Pathologists
Our faculty members hold postgraduate qualifications in pathology and have hands-on experience with FRCPath and NEET SS examinations. They bring real-world diagnostic insight into every lecture, making complex topics approachable and exam-relevant.

### Teaching Philosophy
We believe in focused, high-yield teaching. Every lecture is crafted to maximize retention and exam performance — no fluff, just what matters most for your success.

### Commitment to Students
Our teachers are actively involved in doubt-clearing sessions and stay accessible to students throughout their preparation journey.
