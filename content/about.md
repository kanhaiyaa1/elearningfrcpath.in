## About ElearningFRCPath

ElearningFRCPath is a dedicated online learning platform for pathology professionals and postgraduate trainees preparing for high-stakes examinations — the **FRCPath** (Fellowship of the Royal College of Pathologists, UK) and **NEET SS Pathology** (India).

### Our Mission

To provide accessible, high-quality, exam-focused pathology education that empowers candidates to clear their examinations with confidence.

### What We Offer

- Structured video lectures covering the complete syllabus
- High-yield notes and revision material
- Image-based and MCQ practice
- Regular updates aligned with the latest WHO classifications and examination patterns
- Doubt-clearing sessions with expert faculty

### Why Choose Us

Our content is created by experienced pathologists who understand the demands of these examinations from the inside. We focus on what matters — clinically relevant, exam-targeted teaching that saves you time and helps you score.
